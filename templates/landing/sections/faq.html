Product & Value

AlteraSF is an AI-powered hiring intelligence platform that extracts claims from resumes, generates challenge questions, and scores technical relevance so you can screen faster and hire smarter.

AlteraSF is an AI-first hiring intelligence platform designed to help teams verify resume claims, assess job fit, and surface real signal earlier in the hiring process.

We focus on reducing recruiter workload and interview waste by identifying exaggerated or unsupported claims before they reach the interview loop.

AlteraSF helps hiring teams distinguish credible signal from resume noise. As resumes become increasingly optimized for keywords and AI tools, it is harder to assess whether claims are credible and relevant. AlteraSF evaluates claim plausibility & job fit early in the hiring process, helping teams focus interviews on what actually matters.

How
it Works

Claim Validity is our proprietary approach to assessing whether a resume statement is:

  • Technically coherent
  • Internally consistent
  • Aligned with claimed role, industry, and experience patterns

AlteraSF's system analyzes:

  • Resume claims
  • Role requirements
  • Experience level expectations

It then generates claim-specific challenge questions that test whether a candidate can credibly explain or defend what they listed.

These questions are designed to be:

  • Non-trivial
  • Hard to bluff
  • Relevant to candidate's real job execution
