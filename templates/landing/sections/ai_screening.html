How it Works

AI Screening. AlteraSF Intelligence.

Instead of ATS keyword matching, candidates are evaluated by Claim Validity (whether answers to AI-generated questions match the resume) and Relevancy (how well the resume matches the job description). AlteraSF intelligently highlights key claims in a resume and instantly suggests tailored follow-up questions – making screening faster, smarter, and deeply contextual.

1) Resume Text Highlighting:

AlteraSF reads the resume and identifies key factual claims (i.e. job titles, achievements, skills). It then highlights relevant text segments that contain these claims, marking them as points of interest.

2) Automatic Question Generation:

For each highlighted claim, the system generates smart, tailored questions. These questions are automatically linked to the highlighted text and shown contextually in the interface.
