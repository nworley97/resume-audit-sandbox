# Senior Product Designer

Transform how teams build resumés and hiring workflows as the first dedicated Product Designer at **Altera**.

## About Altera

- YC-backed (W24), fully remote team of 12
- Building AI copilots for talent acquisition teams across North America
- Served 40+ high-growth startups in the last 12 months

## The Opportunity

You'll partner with PM, Eng, and GTM to ship features that help recruiters:

1. Craft compelling job descriptions powered by AI suggestions
2. Collaborate with hiring managers through shared workspaces
3. Track candidate progress and feedback in real time

### What You'll Do

- Lead end-to-end product design for the candidate and recruiter experiences
- Own our design system and contribute to component libraries
- Facilitate customer discovery, usability tests, and design reviews
- Work closely with engineering to deliver pixel-perfect implementations
- Mentor junior designers and champion a design-first mindset

### What We're Looking For

- 5+ years of product design experience, with at least 2 years in SaaS
- Portfolio showcasing shipped product work and iterative process
- Fluency in Figma, prototyping, and storytelling with data
- Comfortable working with ambiguous problem spaces and startup pace

### Bonus Points

- Experience designing productivity tools or recruiting software
- Familiarity with TypeScript, React, or Tailwind
- Strong writing skills and interest in content design
